How Does the Extension Work?

Facebook Activity Scrubber operates entirely within your browser using your existing Facebook session. The extension:

  • Uses Facebook's public web interface that you already have access to
  • Automates the same clicking actions you would perform manually
  • Processes all data locally within your browser
  • Never sends any data to external servers or third parties
  • Stores temporary settings only in your browser's local storage

Local Data Storage

The extension may temporarily store the following data locally in your browser only:

  • Extension settings and preferences
  • Progress tracking information during deletion sessions
  • Temporary state data to resume operations after page refreshes
